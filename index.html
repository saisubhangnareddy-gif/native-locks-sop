Lock Pro — the flashing rule
A technician firmware update (flashing) fixes several Lock Pro issues: mortise not unlocking, lock opening on its own, continuous lock-bell notifications, and speaker noise. Flashing works only on New FP SKU locks — check the reference image. Older SKU locks need a mortise replacement instead.
